Troubleshooting

Help I can't register

If you're having trouble registering, please send an email to help@emplifyapp.com with your details and we'll try and get everything set up on your behalf.

Help I can't login

If you're having trouble logging in, try resetting your password. If that doesn't work email help@emplifyapp.com and we'll take a closer look.

I can't see groups that I'm sure I should have access to

Only administrators can add you to groups.

I've got an idea for a new Group

Please contact your Emplify co-ordinator.
