News

For details on how to post a news article visit the Posting News page.

News is where you can find out the latest from your employer. Article are displayed in reverse chronological order (most recent first).

The news landing page show a summary of the most recent articles, to read more just select any article.

Likes

Clicking like is a way to give positive feedback on a given article. you can see what other people are liking by visiting the Community section of the app.

Comments

You can comment on any article and you comment will be seen by all users beneath the corresponding article and on the Community page
